# Welcome

This is my personal site. \
It is a caricature of my thoughts.

## Why

I've been misunderstood for most of my life.

Partly because my convictions differ so much from what’s considered normal. \
Mostly because of my incapability to express my understanding of the world in simple words.

Perfection has always been an inquietude within me, yet forever beyond reach. \
By definition, perfection is immutable, and I'll always grow, but some ideas are deeply rooted in me.

This is an attempt to understand, aiming at perfection.
