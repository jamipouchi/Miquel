# How I Want to Live My Life

## Why am I writing this

I have a burning desire to do more. To be better. \
I think I have always had it, but it was silenced over the years. Now it's roaring back.

No matter how many times I lose, I know I am going to win.

## How my life has been up to now

When I was young, I got used to being good at everything.

I was talented and lacked ambition, so I didn't put in the effort.

When I went to uni I expected it to be hard. It wasn't.

At that point in my life I couldn't care about anything.\
I was unhappy.

Then I fell in love. I failed hard.\
It made me happy.

Failure was a hard concept to grasp. Now I pursue it deliberately through physical effort.

I have not had a clear path forward. I tumbled from one thing to another until my current job.\
I dedicated a year to it, every day, all day. I am proud of the work done.

But now that I know what I want, I am trapped by its success. I can either wait, or pursue my path and walk away from the reward.

### Physical Effort

I used to think that achieving without effort made me better.

I was wrong. I have fallen in love with pain.\
I now push myself for the effort itself.

### Coding

I came to hate coding.

There has been a breakthrough since. _AI_.

Coding [this site](https://github.com/jamipouchi/Miquel) has been so much fun. Building [fire](https://github.com/embrake-AI/fire) is a joy.

## Happiness

Happiness is a pursuit that dies on its existence.

I have only found it through commitment.

## How I will do it

The only thing that matters is winning.\
We can add artificial constraints, but those are only visible to oneself. 

A goal has to be bold enough to seem unbelievable to most people yet still reachable in your own mind.

This past year I started setting goals. So far I have mostly failed.\
But I will not stop.

Most people live their lives without ever winning.

_I started [tracking my workouts](https://workout-tracker.miquelpuigturon.com/)_

## Dream state

If you don't choose a direction, you will end up living under someone else's dreams.

For most of my life, I’ve been guided by what I didn’t want to do.\
That’s inefficient. There are an infinite number of options to reject, but only one to choose.

My lifetime goals are:

  -  To make a meaningful positive contribution to humanity
  -  To live in complete freedom

It is hard to choose a path that combines both, and I am uncertain how or with which to start.

These goals are not unconditional, they are constrained by _fairness_. \
I want to give back to those who deserve it.

I want to keep the fire burning.

Most days feel like I do something I don’t want, after doing something I didn’t want.

Many people optimize for comfort in the present.\
I have my sight set at eternity.
